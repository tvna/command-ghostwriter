# Decision Handoff

Use this when the human must approve, reject, sequence, defer, choose between prepared owner options, or unblock work.

## Shape

- **Recommendation:** choose one option.
- **Why:** summarize the decisive evidence.
- **Options:** name 2-3 choices.
- **Trade-offs:** cost, risk, reversibility, and what each option preserves.
- **Proof:** link claims to artifacts, commands, URLs, screenshots, or observed behavior.
- **Open Questions:** include only questions that block the decision.

## Quality Bar

Do not ask "what should I do?" until the agent has investigated the available options. If the agent cannot make the decision ready, hand off the blocker and the attempted paths.

## Blocked Variant

- **Blocker:** exact condition stopping progress.
- **Tried:** concrete attempts and observed results.
- **Unknowns:** what remains unverified.
- **Resumption:** what the agent will do after the human chooses.
